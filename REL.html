Bokeh Plot

The selection has been done as such: countries where more than 50% (absolute majority) of the population is either Christian or Muslim is considered religious. The third category is including secular countries and traditional faiths. The values are calculated by dividing the normal GDP by the normal Political Score. It appears secular countries have known an earlier and steeper boom of prosperity than the other, while Muslim countries are the ones lagging the most behind, there are many possible explanations to this, such as is that most of the natural resources present in Africa are in countries that have a majority of Christian people, and on top of this, they are more politically unstable; which is untrue since Maghreb is a petroleum region . Others could see a culture that does not foster good market development or peace; which can be rebutted by looking at the Islamic Golden Age.
